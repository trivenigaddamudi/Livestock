Livestock Management & Health Monitoring

We build smart IoT solutions to monitor animal actions and health in real time—helping farmers prevent disease, improve welfare, and increase productivity.

Company: INFILINK Team: IoT Visioners Domain: Livestock Management

What We Do

  • Wearables to track temperature, activity, rumination
  • Early disease detection & anomaly alerts
  • GPS-based movement & geofencing
  • Dashboards and mobile notifications

Why It Matters

  • Reduce losses from undetected illness
  • Optimize breeding and feeding cycles
  • Increase milk/meat yield with data-driven care
  • Timely interventions to protect herds

Our Vision

A connected, data-driven future for agriculture where every animal’s wellbeing is visible, actionable, and continuously improving.
